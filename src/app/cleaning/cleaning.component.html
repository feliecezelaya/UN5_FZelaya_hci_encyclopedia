Cleaning Your Instrument

Your Steel Drums will be sparkling with these cleaning tips:

  1. Wipe your instrument thoroughly with a smooth, dry cloth that will cause no scratches.
  2. Dip another dry cloth in turtle wax and distribute liberally to the entier drum.
  3. Let the turtle wax dry for 10 to 15 minutes.
  4. With a clean, dry cloth, wipe the instrument until all the turtle wax is removed.
  5. Step back and admire your sparkling clean Steel Drum!
