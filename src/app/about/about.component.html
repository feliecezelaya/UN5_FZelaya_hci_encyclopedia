About the HCI Encyclopedia

The HCI Encyclopedia is meant to provide a single repository for information about Steel Drums.

The Steel Drum is a music instrument known throughout the Caribbean as well as other parts of the world including Japan, Taiwan, and the United States of America.

This application further seeks to increase the awareness of the Steel Drum instrument in hopes that it will encourage more people to learn to play the instrument.
